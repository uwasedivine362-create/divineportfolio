Uwase Divine’s Portfolio is a modern, clean React-based website that showcases your work and skills. It includes:

Home Section – Your name, a short bio, and profile picture.

Projects Section – Cards displaying your projects with images, descriptions, and links.

Skills Section – Highlights your technical skills.

Contact Section – A simple form or links to reach you via email or social media.

Styling – Responsive and visually appealing design that works on desktops and mobile devices.

Basically, it’s a one-stop place for anyone to see who you are, what you can do, and how to contact you.
